about our project

The Increase In The Rate Of Rain Is A Big Problem That We Must Highlight, And The Main Reason For This Problem Is The Phenomenon Of Global Warming, Which Leads To A Rise In Temperature, Which Causes Torrential Rains That Cause The Collapse Of Some Houses And The Disruption Of Roads. How To Detect Climate Change In A Specific Area And How To Apply IOT In Transferring Data From The Waterproof Temperature Sensor And Weight Sensor To Our Website Application Is The Primary Mission.
